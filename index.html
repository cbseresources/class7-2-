Mridula Dwivedi

Mridula Dwivedi has been writing on her blog Travel Tales from India for almost 12 years now. It began with just articles about travel in India, but as she has expanded her horizon since she gave up her academic career and decided to focus solely on writing, her blog now covers articles from her personal travels outwith India.

Dheeraj Sharma

This well-designed and nicely-compiled blog by travelling guru Dheeraj Sharma is dedicated to the beauty of the high and mighty Himalayas of India. Given how gently he writes about the grace of snow-capped peaks and valleys, you might be surprised to find that Sharma’s dayjob is in software!

Indrani Ghose

Indrani Ghose is a top travel blogger India. An ex-Indian Air Force employee, who once dreamed of traveling around the globe. And to be honest, thanks to my job, I did that quite a bit. Then life caught up, and soon mine became a little family of four. And along with motherhood, came the roller-coaster rides that hardly left time for much else. But as they say, once you’re bitten by the travel bug, it really never gets out of your system. So as my little girls grew up, I started to document my journeys through writing and photography.
